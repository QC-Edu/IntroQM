3. ü™ô Doubling the wavelength of radiation doubles its frequency. (True/False)

Answer: False

The wavelength, \(\lambda\), of light is related to frequency, \(\nu\) by the equation \(\nu = \frac{c}{\lambda}\). So doubling the wavelength halves the frequency.

7. üßÆ Two quantum particles move at the same speed, but particle 2 has half the mass of particle 1. The De Broglie wavelength of particle 2 is greater than that of particle 1 by a factor of ____?

Answer: 2

The De Broglie wavelength is \(\lambda = \frac{h}{p}\). Halving the mass of a particle halves its momentum and doubles its wavelength.
